MNM Platform for our Talent

Every talents can upload photos as their portfolio in the MNM app. Every photo they upload will automatically become NFT.

This NFT can be purchased by anyone, and of course the artist can set a price for each NFT.

Even every NFT that has been sold will always give the artist a royalty fee.

So this will be another income besides the likes they collect in the form of MNM coins.

MNM Platform for fans

We offer users to be able to enjoy the content presented by the talents in the MnM application. and, users can directly message talent.

Each fan can give likes and MNM coins to their idol, and the idol will respond a chat from fans in order to receive the gift.

Every fans can also buy NFT from their idol, and from this NFT they can get passive income.

Because for every likes that those NFT photo will get, 90% of the MNM coins received will be obtained by the NFT owner and 10% will be distributed to the NFT creator..
